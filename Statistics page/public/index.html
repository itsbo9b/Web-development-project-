About Us

Qatar University (QU) is the nation’s first higher education institution, established in 1973. Located in Doha, QU is committed to academic excellence, research innovation, and community engagement. With over 20,000 students and a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across ten colleges, we strive to prepare leaders who contribute to Qatar’s National Vision 2030.

Mission

To provide high-quality education, advance knowledge through research, and serve the community.

Vision

To be a model university in the region, recognized for innovation and global impact.

Values

Integrity, excellence, diversity, and sustainability guide our every endeavor.
